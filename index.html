The Process

How jAIstifier Works

A lightweight SDK + SaaS layer that wraps around your AI model — zero retraining, instant explainability.

01
🤖

AI Model Decides

Your existing AI model runs as normal — loan approvals, insurance claims, HR decisions, credit scoring. No changes to the model itself.

→
02
🛡️

jAIstifier SDK Intercepts

The jAIstifier SDK wraps the model locally — it perturbs inputs, re-runs the model, and computes real SHAP feature importance weights.

→
03
📝

Why Was This Decided?

SHAP/LIME analysis runs in real time — identifying which features drove the decision, their weights, and an LLM converts this to plain language.

→
04
📁

Audit Log Stored

Every decision, its explanation, and its timestamp is stored in a tamper-evident audit log — GDPR, EU AI Act, and RBI compliant.

Why This Matters

This isn't a hypothetical. This happened to a real family — and it's happening across millions of AI-driven decisions every day.

A family admitted their 78-year-old grandmother to a hospital. The bill was ₹2,00,000. They applied for a cashless insurance claim. The AI approved ₹1,00,000 and stated "patient liability: ₹3,000" — but never explained where the remaining ₹97,000 went. The hospital refused to discharge her. The family was forced to pay the full amount out of pocket. When they complained to the insurer, even the insurance company couldn't explain the AI's calculation.
